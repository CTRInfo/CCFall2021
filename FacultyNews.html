Grant Award Spotlight

Addressing Adolescent Relationship Abuse (ARA) in a Pediatric Emergency Department (ED) - Funding Agency: Department of Health and Human Services | Centers for Disease Control and Prevention)

Background: 
Because of the high prevalence of ARA among ED patients, the negative long-term health impacts of ARA, and that urban EDs serve as a safety net for high-risk adolescents, the ED can serve as a strategic venue for healthy relationship promotion and identification of and intervention for victims of ARA.

Impact: Stakeholder-informed potential intervention strategies will inform development of ED-based, evidence-informed ARA interventions among high-risk adolescents presenting to a pediatric ED.
